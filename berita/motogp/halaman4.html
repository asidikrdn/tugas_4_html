Halaman 4 - MOTO GP

Dovizioso : The attendance king

The Italian, along with teammate Jirge Lorenzo, has been competing on the World stage of 16 straight years - the third higest on the grid behind Valentino Rossi (Movistar Yamaha MotoGP) and Dani Pedrosa (Repsol Honda Team). It's a huge testament to the 32-years-old, who in the last two years has been - on paper - Marc Marquez' (Repsol Honda Team) most consistent challenger.
